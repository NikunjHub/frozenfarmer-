Our Story

Rooted in family, grown with love, and served with a mission — welcome to The Frozen Farmer.

From Farm to Freezer

The Frozen Farmer was founded on our third-generation family farm in Bridgeville, Delaware. We saw an opportunity to fight food waste by using perfectly imperfect fruits—fruits that are just as delicious but not pretty enough for grocery stores—and turn them into something magical: frozen desserts!

Meet the Founder

Katey Evans, a farmer’s wife and mom, combined her passion for farming and culinary creativity to launch The Frozen Farmer. With support from her family and community, the idea blossomed into a nationally recognized brand that’s making dessert more sustainable.

Making Waves on Shark Tank

In 2020, Katey pitched The Frozen Farmer on ABC’s Shark Tank and scored a deal with Lori Greiner! Since then, the brand has skyrocketed into grocery stores across the country.

Our Mission

We’re more than just a sweet treat — we’re a mission to reduce food waste and deliver healthy, wholesome, delicious desserts made from real ingredients, right from our farm.

Our Journey

Founded by Katey Evans, The Frozen Farmer began as a solution to reduce food waste on our third-generation family farm. Today, we craft frozen desserts that are as sustainable as they are delicious.

Certified Women-Owned Business

The Frozen Farmer is proudly certified as a women-owned business, led by our passionate founder Katey Evans. Her leadership not only inspires our team, but also helps pave the way for women entrepreneurs in agriculture and food innovation.
