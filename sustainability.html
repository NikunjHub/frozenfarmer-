Our Commitment to Sustainability

Turning perfectly imperfect produce into planet-friendly frozen desserts.

Fighting Food Waste

At The Frozen Farmer, we believe that every piece of fruit has value — even the ones that don’t look picture-perfect. That’s why we use “imperfect” fruits that grocery stores would reject and turn them into delicious desserts instead of letting them go to waste.

What is Imperfect Produce?

Imperfect produce refers to fruits and vegetables that are too small, oddly shaped, or slightly blemished. They’re just as tasty and nutritious — but often get discarded. We give them a second chance at life in every scoop.

Locally Grown, Minimally Processed

We grow much of our fruit right on our family farm in Bridgeville, Delaware, and source additional produce locally whenever possible. Our frozen treats are made with simple, farm-fresh ingredients and without artificial preservatives.

From Farm to Freezer with Purpose

Every cup of The Frozen Farmer sorbet or Frobert™ is a small step toward a more sustainable food system. By choosing us, you’re not just enjoying a treat — you’re supporting a future where less food is wasted and more is cherished.
